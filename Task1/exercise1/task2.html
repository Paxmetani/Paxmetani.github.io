Mukhtar Auezov

About Mukhtar

Mukhtar Omarkhanovich Auezov (1897–1961) was a Kazakh writer, a social activist, a Doctor of Philology, and an honored academician of the Soviet Union (1946).

Key Life Events

  1. 1897 – Born in Semipalatinsk Oblast, Russian Empire (now in Abay District, Kazakhstan)
  2. 1917 – Auezov married Raihan
  3. 1942 – The first book "Abai Joly" was published
  4. 1949 - Two books of "Abai" won the first level award of USSR
  5. 1961 – died during an operation in Moscow

Main Contributions

  • «Path of Abai»
  • «Aiman-Sholpan»
  • «Enilik-Kebek»
  • Sсientific contribution in kazakh culture and literature
